FAQ

What is a hackathon?

Put simply, a hackathon is a chance to build whatever you want. Have an idea? The Dons Hack is the place to make it a reality. A hackathon gives you the opportunity to build your portfolio, develop skills, and learn how to work in a team.

Who can participate?

All USFCA students can participate regardless of major or gender. No prior coding or technical experience is necessary!

Is The Dons Hack fully in-person this year?

Yes! The Dons Hack will be in-person this year! It will be located on campus (check the schedule for more specific locations). Workspace rooms will also be available overnight.

How much does it cost?

The Dons Hack is free for our student attendees! Food and light refreshments will be provided.

Do I need to have a team to apply? Is there a limit to the size of each team?

It is not necessary to have a team at the time of registration. We will help you form a team. If you choose to build a team, there can be a maximum of 4 people.
